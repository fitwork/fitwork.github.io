Strength training program

Strength training is an essential part of a quality exercise program.

Strength training improves posture, reduces the possibility of joint injury, improves body composition, prevents osteoporosis, reduces loss of muscle mass as we age and helps to ensure daily activities are met with ease.

Program Goals

Strength

Cardio

Functionality

Body and Mind

Beginner Strength Training Program

Perfect for someone new to exercise or with an inactive lifestyle. The beginner strength training program will start with a warm up, 10-15 min cardio and provide an introduction to basic strength training techniques, proper form and strength training equipment. Beginner strength training focus will be on increasing muscular endurance. Full body engagement can be expected to ensure core strength is improved providing more stabilization for progression into intermediate strength training. Equipment may include weight machines, a stability ball, hydro (water) resistance and body weight exercises. The beginner strength training program will also include stretching and a mind and body component comprised of a personal reflection of the completed session.

  • Duration: 60 Minutes
  • Level: Easy

Intermediate Strength Training Program

Perfect for someone who exercises on their own or someone who lives a somewhat active lifestyle. The intermediate strength training program will start with a warm up, 10-15 minutes cardio and provides advancement into more challenging strength training techniques. Intermediate strength training focus will be on increasing muscular strength and incorporating strength training exercise variety. Equipment may include weight resistance machines, cable machines, free weights, stability balls, bosu balls and various body weight resistance exercises. The intermediate strength training program will also include stretching and a mind and body component comprised of a personal reflection of the completed session.

  • Duration: 60 Minutes
  • Level: Challenging

Advanced Strength Training Program

Perfect for someone who regularly participates in exercise classes, who may go to a gym to exercise regularly or may be involved in sports. Someone with a very active lifestyle. The advanced strength training program starts with a warm up, 10-15 minutes cardio and provides a structured strength training program with focus on high intensity strength training methods required and necessary for additional physical improvements, increasing muscular size, increasing strength training duration, repetitions and sets. The advanced training program includes muscle specific strength training to ensure muscle balance is achieved for sport specific individuals and to increase power for the sport. In addition, you can expect to maximize core strength. The advanced strength training program also includes stretching, referrals to registered massage therapists for further benefits. The mind and body component will include visualization techniques, referrals to registered dietitians if required to encompass nutritional requirements and is a personal reflection upon the completed session.

  • Duration: 60 Minutes
  • Level: Difficult
