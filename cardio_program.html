Cardio program

There are many benefits of having a healthy strong heart. Benefits include a lower resting heart rate, lower risk of having heart disease, an increase in blood volume to the rest of the body and most importantly, it reduces the risk of death.

Toning

Beginner Cardio Program

Perfect for someone new to an exercise program or with an inactive lifestyle. The beginner cardio program will start with a warm up and provide an introduction to different cardio options. This is a low intensity program with focus on slowly increasing the duration of cardio time. The toning component is comprised of an introduction to basic resistance (strength) training methods. The beginner cardio program will also include a cool down with stretching. The mind and body component is a personal reflection upon the completed session.

Low intensity
  • Duration: 60 Minutes
  • Level: Beginner

Intermediate Cardio Program

Perfect for someone who already exercises on their own or someone who lives a somewhat active lifestyle. The intermediate cardio program will start with a warm up and provide knowledge on how to increase cardio exercise intensity, improve the results of current cardio, how to exercise within target heart rate zones, goal is to increase weight loss. This is a moderate intensity program with focus on increasing frequency and duration of time. The toning component provides a wide range of resistance (strength) training methods with focus on increasing frequency. The intermediate program will also include a cool down with muscle specific stretching. The mind and body component is a personal reflection upon the completed session.

Moderate Intensity
  • Duration: 60 Minutes
  • Level: Intermediate

Advanced Cardio Program

Perfect for someone who regularly participates in exercise classes, who may go to a gym to exercise regularly or be involved in sports. Someone with a very active lifestyle. The advanced cardio program will start with a warm up and provide structured program designed to increase performance, heart rate maximums, high intensity interval training, circuit training and other types of advanced cardio. This is a high intensity program with focus on shorter durations, it is also interchanged with moderate and lower intensities to engage in full programing. The toning component consists of exercises completed in conjunction with cardio components in order to provide the intensity level required and necessary for additional physical improvements. Muscle specific toning to ensure muscle balance is achieved for sport specific individuals and to increase power for the sport. The advanced program also includes a cool down, stretching and referrals to registered massage therapists for further benefits. The mind and Body component will include visualization techniques, referrals to registered dietitians if required to encompass nutritional requirements and is a personal reflection upon the completed session.

High Intensity
  • Duration: 60 Minutes
  • Level: Advanced
