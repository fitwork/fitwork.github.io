CALORIES BURNED BY ACTIVITY

When beginning a new exercise program it is beneficial to analyze your activity levels over a 24 hour period three-four days a week.

This will provide an indication of how active you are and where improvements can be incorporated into your current lifestyle helping you to become more successful in reaching your fitness goals. We burn calories even when we sleep! Balancing food intake and physical activity will help to maintain a healthy weight.
